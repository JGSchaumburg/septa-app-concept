SEPTA App — Descoped Concept

A clickable concept for a SEPTA app scoped to what only SEPTA can own and what one developer can sustain. The five tabs are the strategy: keep fare, regional rail, official alerts, and the high-value views the redesign dropped; hand general trip planning to a Transit partnership.

Tap the bottom tabs to move between screens. Tap a saved trip on Home to expand it. Orange dashed cards are strategy annotations, not part of the UI — toggle them off to see the clean app.
